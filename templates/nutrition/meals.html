Simple Meal Plans and Snack Options

Below is a simple meal plan for the day before a game and for the day of a game.

The day before a big game/event is where you get all your fueling done.

The calorie count for these meal are below them. You dont have to hit the calorie count exactly,
but you should be near enough to them.
All the meals are open to change. Just ensure you substitute in something of the same nutritional benefit.


Meal Plan Day Before a Match

Ask any nutritionist and they'll tell you the same.
The day before a match/exercise, is where all your fueling and preparation are done.
Here I'll show you a simple meal plan for the day BEFORE a match.
Breakfast
- 1 bowl of Porridge: 100g.
- 1 small glass of orange juice: 200ml
- 1 small white roll (with jam ideally)
Snack 1
- 1 small pot of flavoured Muller Rice
- 1 pint of milk
- 1 medium banana.
Lunch
- 1 bag of Brown Rice: 250g.
- 1 Serving of vegetables.
- 1 breast of grilled chicken: 130g
- 1 small white roll.
- OPTIONAL 1 homemade protein shake (1 scope)
Snack 2
- 1 cereal Bar: 60g
- 1 glass of milk
Dinner
- 1 Serving of Potatoes: 250g.
- 1 breast of chicken (grilled/fillet): 130g
- Serving of Vegetables
- 1 side of rice pudding or custard.
Pre-Bed
- Cottage Cheese: 230g
- 1 wedge slice of Pineapple.
- Dark Cgocolate: 3 squares.
- 1 Slice of Brown/White Toast

Total Calories: 2,765

Meal Plan for Match Day

The day of match, it is vital to get a good breakfast on board.
Once a good breakfast is had, the rest of the day is just topping up the body for the game.
The following is a simple game day meal plan:

Breakfast
- 1 bowl of porridge. 100-150g
- Natural yogurt with a serving of fruit.
- 1 glass of orange juice.

Pre-Match Meal Option 1
- 2 medium sized eggs.
- 2 slices of brown bread.
- I glass of a fruit smoothie.
Total Calories: 843 (Breakfast included)

Pre-Match Meal Option 2
- 1 Breast of chicken.
- 1 serving of vegetables.
- 1 glass of juice.
Total Calories: 760 (Breakfast included)

Snack Options

Below is a list of snacks that are ideal for general day/match day eating:

- Fruit (Apples/Bananas/Oranges/Grapes/Pineapple)
- Natural Yougurt (Avoid flavoured unless it is greek style)
- Cereal bars.
- Protein Bars
- Mixed Nuts (peanuts, cashnuts and almonds)
- Dark Chocolate
